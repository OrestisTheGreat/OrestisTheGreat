Orestis II

Can a modern civilization be deliberately designed?
Can Greece be governed not for the next election cycle, but for the next century?

Orestis II of Greece, born as Orestis Mastakas and also known as Orestis the Great, is the founder of Orestisism and claimant to the throne of Greece.

This archive documents his life, campaigns, political doctrine, and long-term vision for the restoration of Greece and the creation of a Fourth Hellenic Civilization.

The political philosophy presented throughout this archive is entirely serious. Its presentation through the language of a royal archive is a literary and historical framing device used throughout the website.
