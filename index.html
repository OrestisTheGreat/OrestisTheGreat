Orestis II

Can a modern civilization be deliberately designed?
Can Greece be governed not for the next election cycle, but for the next century?

Orestis II of Greece, born as Orestis Mastakas and also known as Orestis the Great, is the founder of Orestisism and claimant to the throne of Greece.

This website combines two complementary narratives. It presents the Restoration Program and Orestisism as a serious political project for the long-term renewal of Greece, while documenting the life, campaigns, and public activities of Orestis II through the literary style of a royal household and historical archive.
