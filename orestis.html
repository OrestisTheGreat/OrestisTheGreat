Orestis II

The Uncrowned King of the Nerds

Orestis II was born in Athens and devoted much of his life to scholarship and institutional campaigns.

Birth and Omens

Born in Athens, Greece, on 21 July 1995.

Shares his birthday with Alexander the Great. Court chroniclers have also noted that both births coincided with the outbreak of a major fire: the destruction of the Temple of Artemis at Ephesus in one case, and the Parnitha wildfire in the other. Historians continue to debate whether this is a coincidence.

Early Signs of Excessive Studiousness

  • Highest average grade at middle school: 20.0 / 20.
  • Highest average grade at high school: 19.8 / 20.
  • Panhellenic examination score: 19,553 points, ranking among the highest nationwide in the technological track.

The Engineering Years

Studied Electrical and Computer Engineering at the National Technical University of Athens. Graduated with a GPA of 9.54 / 10 (top 1%), despite never fully reconciling himself with the discipline.

The Swiss Interlude

Pursued doctoral studies in Management at the University of St. Gallen, during what court historians maintain was its final era as a global top-30 university.

Courtly Interests

  • Favorite (and only) sport: Chess. Peak online rating: 2467 ELO.

A Note on the Numbering of Kings

The title "Orestis II" is adopted in honor of Orestes, an obscure king of ancient Macedon, whose reign is imperfectly documented and whose achievements remain the subject of limited scholarly enthusiasm.
